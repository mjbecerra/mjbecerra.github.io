**Organization:** Centro Amazónico de Investigación y Control de Enfermedades Tropicales (CAICET) (November 2017 – January 2018)

**Role:** Cartographer

Construction of a Geographic Information System (GIS) to map and analyze the impact of tropical diseases — particularly malaria — in the Venezuelan Amazon. The project applied participatory cartographic methodologies to identify disease foci in Atures Municipality, Amazonas State.
