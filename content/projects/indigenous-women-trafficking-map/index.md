**Organization:** Organización de mujeres indígenas amazónicas Wanaaleru (2015 – 2016)

**Role:** Technical Advisor

Design and application of methodologies for mapping illegal gold mining operations in the Venezuelan Amazon, with a focus on documenting indigenous women trafficking for sexual exploitation. The project produced a social map to support advocacy and territorial rights of indigenous communities.
