**Organization:** Grupo de Estudios Antropológicos, Ciudad Bolívar, Venezuela (January 2013 – December 2013)

**Role:** Guest Researcher

Development of methodologies for georeferenced epidemiological data collection among the Anú, Barí, Cuiva, Hiwi, and Wayúu peoples. The project produced cartographic representations of epidemiological data and assessed the coverage and reach of the public health system in indigenous territories.
