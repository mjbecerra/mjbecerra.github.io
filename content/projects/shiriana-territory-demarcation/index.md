**Organization:** Escuela de Antropología, Universidad Central de Venezuela (January 2015 – December 2016)

**Role:** Technical Team Member

Design and application of cartographic survey methodologies to support the self-demarcation process of the Shiriana people's traditional and ancestral territory. The project was framed within an anthropological advisory initiative for endogenous projects in indigenous communities, combining geographic and anthropological approaches.
